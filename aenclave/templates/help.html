General Instructions

Audio Enclave is pretty self explanatory. Click upload to upload songs, search for them, and play them.

Playlists

Playlists can be constructed with the buttons on the bottom panel. Favorites playlists are a special kind of playlist that you can easily add to and remove from by clicking the heart icon. If you don't see the heart icons, make sure that you are logged in.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • j/k - move selection up or down
  • enter - queue the selected song
  • a - toggle select all on the page
  • h - go home
  • f - go to my favorites
  • p - go to playlists
  • c - go to channels
  • b - go to browse
  • u - go to upload
  • r - go to roulette

Contact Maintainers

Audio Enclave is part of the Media-Enclave project, which is hosted on Google Code. You can file bugs here, and send email to men_clave-d_ev at mit dot edu (without underscores).
